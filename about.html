Company Overview

Stellar Silk Road International Trade Co., Ltd. is located in Tangshan, China. We are a comprehensive enterprise focused on global trade services. Our main business sectors include solar appliances, steel products, footwear and apparel, pet supplies, packaging materials, and cycling accessories. Backed by China's strong industrial base and inspired by the Belt and Road Initiative, our vision is to connect with every corner of the world and deliver high-quality and affordable Chinese industrial products globally.

Our Advantages

Located in Tangshan, Hebei Province, China’s top economic city in the region, the company benefits from a strong location advantage and convenient transportation. In 2024, the city's GDP exceeded one trillion yuan, and it sits within the one-hour economic zone of Beijing, maintaining close economic cooperation with the capital.

Tangshan boasts excellent import and export infrastructure, with access to several ports such as Jingtang and Caofeidian. As of 2025, Tangshan Port Group has become the second-largest in the world in terms of cargo throughput.

Tangshan also has a solid industrial foundation, ranking among the world’s top steel producers. Its finished industrial goods and steel-related products have a significant competitive advantage.

Historical Background

The history of Tangshan dates back to the Tang Dynasty in 645 AD, when Emperor Li Shimin marched eastward to this area. The city was named after a mountain ("Tangshan") located here. Over more than a thousand years of development, Tangshan has become a key city in northern China.
